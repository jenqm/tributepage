Rusty

Here's a time line of Rusty's life:

  • July 2003 - was adopted
  • November 2003 - learns to give paw for treats.
  • 2004 - starts going blind.
  • 2005 - becomes severely allergic to fleas.
  • 2007 - moves to Costa Rica. His mom's birth country.
  • 2009 - gets a new sister
  • 2010 - visits lots of beaches
  • 2011 - couldn't stay off sister, gets balls cut off.
  • 2014 - moves out of grandmother's house
  • 2016 - moves in with stepdad
  • 2017 - dies at the age of 14
